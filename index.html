Key Information

The Toronto International Film Festival returns September 5 – 15, 2024 for its 49th edition — 11 days of Canadian and international cinema; special events and talks with some of the biggest names in film; and TIFF's Industry Conference: a place to connect with film professionals and explore the art and business of cinema.

Plan your visit

  • Subway

    TIFF Lightbox is closest to St. Andrew subway station on Line 1. TIFF is a 450m or 6 minute walk west from the subway.

  • Streetcar or Bus

    Take the King streetcar (Route 504). The closest stop is John St. This will bring you directly in front of TIFF Lightbox.

  • GO Transit or Via Rail

    Take a GO Train, GO Bus or Via Rail train into Union Station, then a 15min walk or transfer to TTC subway (St. Andrew stop) to TIFF Lightbox.

Get Tickets

Prices do not apply to the Toronto International Film Festival presented by Rogers. Tickets can be purchased at the Steve & Rashmi Gupta Box Office, via the calendar, or calling 1-888-258-8433.
